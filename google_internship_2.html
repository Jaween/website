Google Internship 2 (YouTube)
2016 August to November

As part of my second internship at Google I implemented the feature to add special effects to image posts on the YouTube Android app.

Content creators on YouTube have access to the “YouTube Community” social networking feature, allowing them to post non-video content to their subscribers. My task was to integrate Google’s visual effects framework into the app as well as implementing a user interface to use the framework.

The resulting feature enables content creators to add filters and special effects to their “YouTube Community” image posts.
