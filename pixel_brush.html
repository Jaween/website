Pixel Brush
2014 October to December

Pixel Brush is a pixel art drawing app for Android. Development was performed by myself in 2014, and continued part time during 2017. It was created to achieve my goal of creating a program similar to Microsoft Paint.

The app was released in open beta in March 2017. As of January 2018, the app has received 61,000 downloads on Google Play.
