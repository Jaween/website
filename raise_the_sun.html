Raise The Sun
2016 January

Raise the Sun is a short single-player experience created in a team of six over a weekend as part of the Global Game Jam 2016. The theme of this game jam was ‘Ritual’. The team consisted of three programmers, two artists and a game designer. The team over scoped the game during the initial design phase and consequently had to cut features during the 48 hour development period, resulting in this short, atmospheric oddity.
