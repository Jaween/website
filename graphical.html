Graphical
2012 July to 2013 June

A calculator app to graph functions. Graphical was developed during my final year of high school and my first year of university. The app was designed and developed by me as a way to learn app development and because I wanted to create a free graphing calculator.

The app includes a maths parser, arithmetic evaluator, simple grapher, numerical integration (unreleased), value-tracing and a dedicated maths input keyboard.
